Hikvision announces new AX Hybrid PRO alarm system with innovative ‘Speed-X’ technology.

How many devices does the Hikvision Ax pro alarm support?

The Hikvision Ax Pro Alarm supports up to 96 devices in a combination of wireless detection devices or wireless outputs. It forms an integral part of the Hik-Connect all in one Eco-System. The hub incorporates Wi-Fi, TCP/IP, and GPRS/3G/4G communication. The AX Pro system provides advanced detection for comprehensive protection.

Wanting to know more about Ax pro's system?

There are two motion alarm detectors in the AX PRO range. The first is a standard unit that detects door-open ‘gaps’ of up to 43mm and a slimline, more discreet unit that detects door-open gaps of up to 53mm.
